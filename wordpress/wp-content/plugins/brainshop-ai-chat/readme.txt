Engage your visitors with BrainShop AI chat. It's free.

== Description ==

Whether you use WordPress to publish personal blogs or sell services or goods online, you should try this plugin.

This plugin adds BrainShop AI chat to your website, and enables you to enjoy the benefits below immediately.

*   Engage visisitors and keep them on your website.
*   Get questions about you or your blog/service/product answered.
*   Understand what visitors think by reading through chat logs.
*   Boost newsletter signup or social media following.
*   Turn more visitors into buying clients (conversion rate optimization).

    BrainShop enables you to empower your WordPress with advanced AI. It's simple to intall and easy to use. The best is, it's free!

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/brainshop` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
1. Use the Settings->BrainShop screen to configure the plugin.
1. Following the instructions on the settings screen to complete installation.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is BrainShop free?  =

Yes, BrainShop includes a free plan that allows you to use AI chat without a volume limit.

= Shall I use real-person live chat instead of a chat bot =

Yes if you have time or resources to provide 24-7 live chat. We're making significant progress with AI but human still excels AI in serveing human. However, if real-person live chat is not affordable to you, we strongly encourage you to install this plugin and use the power of AI to bring your website to a higher level.

= How does BrainShop compare to Siri, Cortana or Xiaoice? =

Siri, Cortana or Xiaoice might be good but they simplly cannot answer questions about you or your website. If you really want to make a comparison, go to http://play.google.com and search for the following apps.

*    Learn English with Aco
*    Cortana
*    Xiaoice 

"Learn English with Aco" is an endication app powered by same AI technologies behind BrainShop.
